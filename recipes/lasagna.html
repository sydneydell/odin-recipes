Lasagna Recipe

Description

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below. Here are the basics:

Step-by-Step Overview

Here's a brief overview of what you can expect when you make homemade lasagna:

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving

Layering the Lasagna

Here is the order you'll follow:

  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta Mixture
  4. Mozarella Slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat the layers, the top with the remaining Parmesan

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • ¾ pound lean ground beef
  • ½ cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • ½ cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1 ½ teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt, divided, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • ½ teaspoon fennel seeds
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • ¾ pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Gather all your ingredients for making lasagna on a kitchen countertop.
  2. Cook 1 pound sweet Italian sausage, ¾ pound lean ground beef, ½ cup minced onion, and 2 cloves garlic, crushed, in a Dutch oven over medium heat until well browned.
  3. Stir in 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes, 2 (6.5 ounce) cans tomato sauce, 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste, and ½ cup water. Season with 2 tablespoons white sugar, 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley (divided), 1 ½ teaspoons dried basil leaves, 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning, ½ teaspoon fennel seeds, ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper, and 1 ½ teaspoons salt (divided, or to taste). Simmer, covered, for about 1 ½ hours, stirring occasionally.
  4. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook 12 lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain the noodles and rinse with cold water.
  5. In a mixing bowl, combine 16 ounces ricotta cheese with 1 egg, 2 tablespoons parsley (remaining), and ½ teaspoon salt.
  6. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  7. Spread 1 ½ cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over the meat sauce, overlapping slightly. Spread ½ of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles. Top with 1/3 of the ¾ pound mozzarella cheese slices. Spoon 1 ½ cups meat sauce over the mozzarella, and sprinkle with ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese.
  8. Repeat layers: add more meat sauce, the remaining mozzarella, and Parmesan cheese. Cover with foil: either spray the foil with cooking spray or ensure it does not touch the cheese.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes.
  10. Let the lasagna rest for 15 minutes before serving.
